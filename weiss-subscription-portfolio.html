Drop-off Analysis & Recommended Actions

Biggest leak: landing page form (68% abandon). A/B test a 1-field email-only capture before showing the full form — projected lift: +35% downloads. Second leak: email open rate (18%). Subject line testing with urgency framing ("Alert: 3 stocks rated SELL today") projected to push opens to 24–26%, adding ~100 paid subs per campaign at current funnel rates.

CAC vs. LTV by Acquisition Channel

Understanding which channels deliver the highest-value subscribers — critical for budget allocation

Budget Recommendation

Organic search and direct mail deliver the best LTV:CAC ratios (18× and 14× respectively). Paid social has a high CAC and below-average LTV — subscribers acquired here show 40% higher 90-day churn. Recommend shifting 20% of paid social budget to SEO content and list-based direct mail for the next quarter.

Real-World Scenario Interpretations
How I would diagnose and respond to these subscription health signals
Scenario A — Churn Spike After Price Increase
MRR holds flat month-over-month, but active subscriber count drops 8% following a $20 annual price increase. New revenue from the increase is masking a churn crisis in the making.
Action: Segment churned users by tenure. If >60% are <6-month subscribers, the price point hit new customers hardest. Introduce a loyalty lock-in offer (grandfather pricing for >12-month subs) and a lower-cost tier to retain price-sensitive leads.
Scenario B — Low Trial-to-Paid Conversion
Trial starts are up 30% from a successful campaign, but conversion to paid has dropped from 65% to 38%. Acquisition cost is rising with no revenue payoff.
Action: Audit the trial onboarding email sequence. Ensure subscribers receive at least one high-value alert or market call during the trial window — for a financial ratings product, value must be demonstrated, not just described. Add a "Your first SELL-rated stock" trigger email on day 3.
Scenario C — Expansion MRR Opportunity
Behavioral data shows 22% of base-tier subscribers are clicking on premium content 5+ times per month, but never upgrading. They are self-selecting as upgrade candidates.
Action: Trigger an in-app upsell modal after the 4th premium content view. Offer a 14-day free upgrade. This segment has demonstrated intent — a soft nudge has historically converted at 2–3× the rate of cold upsell campaigns.
